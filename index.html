Let's help in this important fight

Did you know that, according to the UN, during the year 2019, 17% of the total food available ended up in the garbage cans of homes, retailers, restaurants and other food services. This strong figure shows us how important it is to combat this problem.

Mission

We are part of a web development company committed to creating efficient and innovative web applications, in order to allow our users to access a wide range of options in the food area in an agile and sustainable way.

Vision

As developers of Tasty Point, our vision is to promote a culture of responsible and efficient consumption, where a nutritious dish reaches the home of each of the people who decide to join our membership system. Also demonstrating the potential that Peru has compared to other large companies in the same field.

About us

Tasty Point is a Mobile Application focused on online delivery, with quick access and easy navigation for its users, Tasty Point promises to reduce the percentage of food wasted. For this, our Mobile App brings together different restaurants or food chains to take advantage of their dishes or products that were not consumed, but are in good condition for consumption. With the help of Tasty Point, finding dishes according to the user's preference at a good price is simple and cheap.
